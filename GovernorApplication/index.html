Governor Requirements

Eligibility

Must be at least 30 years old, a U.S. citizen, and a Florida resident for at least 7 years prior to election.

Political Experience

Previous experience in public service, government, or leadership roles is highly recommended.

Documentation

Must provide valid identification, proof of residency, and complete financial disclosure forms.
