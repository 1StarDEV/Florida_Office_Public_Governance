ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS

Florida Governor Eligibility Requirements

Complete list of requirements and qualifications needed to apply for the Governor position

Governor Application Eligibility Criteria

All applicants must meet these requirements to be considered for the Governor position

Requirement Status Legend

Required - Must meet this requirement
Preferred - Strongly recommended but not mandatory
Optional - Helpful but not required

Age & Basic Requirements

Age Requirement

  • Must be at least 13 years of age (Required)
  • Must be able to verify age if requested (Required)
  • No maximum age limit (Optional)

Community Standing

  • Must have a clean roleplay record with no major infractions (Required)
  • No active bans or suspensions from the community (Required)
  • Positive reputation within the roleplay community (Preferred)
  • Active participation in community events (Preferred)

Automatic Disqualification

Applications will be automatically disqualified if the applicant does not meet ALL required criteria listed above. There are no exceptions to these basic requirements.

Platform Requirements

Discord Account

Must have a valid Discord account in good standing with appropriate username and profile

Required

Roblox Account

Must have an active Roblox account with no history of platform bans or suspensions

Required

Activity Level

Regular activity on both Discord and Roblox platforms is expected for this position

Preferred

Platform Specifics

  • Discord account must be at least 30 days old (Required)
  • Roblox account must be at least 60 days old (Required)
  • No shared or borrowed accounts allowed (Required)
  • Must be able to verify ownership of both accounts (Required)
  • Regular activity (3+ times per week) on Discord (Preferred)
  • Regular participation in community servers (Preferred)

Roleplay Experience

Experience Level

  • Minimum 3 months of roleplay experience (Required)
  • Experience with government or leadership roleplay (Preferred)
  • Previous experience in administrative roles (Preferred)
  • Experience with conflict resolution in roleplay (Preferred)
  • 6+ months of consistent roleplay experience (Optional)
  • Experience managing teams or groups (Optional)

Roleplay Record

  • No major roleplay infractions in the past 6 months (Required)
  • No history of metagaming or powergaming (Required)
  • Clean record with no formal warnings (Preferred)
  • Positive feedback from other community members (Preferred)
  • Experience with multiple roleplay genres (Optional)

Experience Verification

All roleplay experience claims may be verified through community records and member feedback. Be prepared to provide examples of your roleplay experience if requested.

Leadership & Skills

Communication

Clear and effective communication skills in both written and verbal forms

Required

Decision Making

Ability to make fair and balanced decisions under pressure

Required

Conflict Resolution

Skills in mediating disputes and finding peaceful solutions

Preferred

Additional Skills

  • Ability to work well in team environments (Required)
  • Basic understanding of government structures (Preferred)
  • Experience with event planning or organization (Preferred)
  • Knowledge of roleplay rules and guidelines (Required)
  • Ability to commit time to the position (Required)
  • Creative problem-solving skills (Preferred)

Time Commitment

Availability Requirements

  • Ability to be active 3-5 times per week (Required)
  • Available for scheduled meetings and events (Required)
  • Can commit to the position for minimum 2 months (Required)
  • Regular check-ins with department staff (Required)
  • Available during peak community hours (Preferred)
  • Flexible schedule for emergency situations (Preferred)

Important Note

The Governor position requires consistent activity and availability. Applicants should carefully consider their current schedule and commitments before applying. Inactivity for more than 7 days without notice may result in removal from the position.

Application Process

Application Requirements

  • Complete all sections of the application form (Required)
  • Provide truthful and accurate information (Required)
  • Use your own original writing - no AI assistance (Required)
  • Submit before the application deadline (Required)
  • Follow all application guidelines (Required)
  • Be available for the 1-2 hour review process (Required)

Strict Prohibitions

Using any AI tools, writing assistants, or external sources to help answer questions or improve your text is strictly forbidden and will result in immediate department blacklisting. All applications must be your own original work.

Verification Process

  • All information provided will be verified (Required)
  • Discord and Roblox accounts will be checked (Required)
  • Roleplay history may be reviewed (Required)
  • Community feedback may be considered (Preferred)
  • Applications reviewed by State Executive Director (Required)

Final Considerations

Ideal Candidate Profile

The ideal Governor candidate demonstrates strong leadership qualities, excellent communication skills, a clean roleplay record, and genuine commitment to the community. They should be active, engaged, and capable of making fair decisions that benefit the entire community.

Review Process

  • Applications reviewed by Jason "Star" Shepherd, State Executive Director
  • Review process takes approximately 1-2 hours after submission
  • All decisions are final and based on submitted applications
  • No interviews or additional questions after submission
  • Notifications sent via Discord upon completion
  • Selected candidates contacted directly for next steps

Need Help?

If you have questions about these requirements or need clarification, please review the Application Guidelines or FAQ pages before contacting the application committee through our Discord server.
