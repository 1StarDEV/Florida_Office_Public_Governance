General Questions

What is the purpose of the Governor position?

The Governor serves as the highest-ranking state official in the Florida roleplay community, responsible for overseeing state operations, managing executive departments, signing legislation into law, and representing the community in official capacities. The Governor reports directly to the State Executive Director.

How long is the Governor's term?

The standard term for Governor is 3 months, after which elections are held for the next term. Governors may run for re-election if they wish to continue serving.

What happens if the Governor becomes inactive?

If a Governor is inactive for more than 7 days without prior notice, they may be subject to removal proceedings. The State Executive Director (SED) would assume temporary duties and oversee the transition until a special election can be held or a replacement is appointed.

Can I apply for other positions while applying for Governor?

Yes, you may apply for other positions simultaneously. However, if selected for multiple roles, you will need to choose which position to accept, as holding multiple high-ranking positions simultaneously is generally not permitted.

Chain of Command

Official Hierarchy

  • 1 County General (Highest Authority)
  • 2 State Executive Director (SED)
  • 3 Governor
  • 4 Lieutenant Governor
  • 5 State Department Heads

The County General holds authority above even the President of the United States in our roleplay hierarchy.

Who does the Governor report to?

The Governor reports directly to the State Executive Director (SED), who is one rank above the Governor in the chain of command. The SED oversees all state operations and has final approval on major gubernatorial decisions.

What is the role of the State Executive Director?

The State Executive Director (SED) is the highest-ranking official at the state level, overseeing all state operations, approving major decisions, and ensuring compliance with community standards. The SED reports directly to the County General.

Who has the final authority in our community?

The County General holds the highest authority in our roleplay community, with power extending above even the President of the United States in our hierarchy. All officials, including the State Executive Director and Governor, ultimately report to the County General.

Eligibility Questions

What are the minimum age requirements?

Applicants must be at least 13 years of age. There is no maximum age limit. Age verification may be requested during the application process.

Do I need previous government roleplay experience?

While previous government or leadership roleplay experience is preferred, it is not strictly required. We value demonstrated leadership qualities, communication skills, and commitment to the community above specific experience.

What if I have a past infraction on my record?

Minor infractions from more than 6 months ago generally won't disqualify you, provided you've maintained a clean record since. Major infractions or recent violations may impact your application. Be honest about your history in your application.

Can I apply if I'm not very active on Discord?

Regular Discord activity is required for the Governor position, as it's our primary communication platform. If selected, you'll need to commit to being active on Discord at least 3-5 times per week for official communications and community engagement.

Application Process

How long does the application take to complete?

The application typically takes 30-60 minutes to complete thoroughly. We recommend setting aside enough time to provide thoughtful, detailed responses to all questions.

Can I save my application and finish it later?

No, the application must be completed in one session. We recommend preparing your responses in a separate document first, then copying them into the application form when you're ready to submit.

What kind of questions are on the application?

The application includes questions about your roleplay experience, leadership philosophy, conflict resolution approaches, and your vision for the community. All questions require original, thoughtful responses.

Important Notice

Using AI tools, writing assistants, or external sources to help answer application questions is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate disqualification and department blacklisting. All responses must be your own original work.

Can I edit my application after submitting?

No, applications cannot be edited once submitted. Please review your responses carefully before final submission.

Review Process

How long does the review process take?

The review process typically takes 1-2 hours after submission. All applications are reviewed by Jason "Star" Shepherd, State Executive Director.

Will I be interviewed after submitting my application?

No, there are no interviews or additional questions after submission. The selection is based entirely on your written application, so make sure it fully represents your qualifications and ideas.

How will I know if I've been selected?

All applicants will receive notification via Discord once the review process is complete. Selected candidates will be contacted directly with next steps.

Can I get feedback if my application is rejected?

Due to the volume of applications, we're unable to provide individual feedback to unsuccessful applicants. However, you're welcome to reapply in future application cycles.

Application Tips

Successful applications typically demonstrate clear communication, thoughtful responses, genuine commitment to the community, and a well-articulated vision for the Governor role. Take your time to craft original, detailed answers that showcase your qualifications.

Technical Issues

What should I do if the application form isn't loading?

First, try refreshing the page. If the problem persists, clear your browser cache and cookies, or try using a different browser. If issues continue, contact the application committee through our Discord server.

What if I experience technical issues during submission?

If you encounter issues during submission, take a screenshot of the error and contact the application committee immediately via Discord. Please include your Discord username and a description of the problem.

I didn't receive a confirmation after submitting. What should I do?

You should receive an automatic confirmation after successful submission. If you don't receive one within 5 minutes, check your spam folder first, then contact the application committee to verify your submission was received.

Still Have Questions?

If your question wasn't answered here, please review the Application Guidelines or contact the application committee through our Discord server. We're happy to help with any additional questions you may have about the Governor application process.
