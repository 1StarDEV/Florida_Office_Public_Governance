Frequently Asked Questions

How can I report government misconduct?
You can report government misconduct through our secure online portal, by calling our hotline at (850) 123-4567, or by visiting any of our regional offices. All reports are confidential, and we have whistleblower protections in place.
What services does the Office provide to citizens?
We provide a wide range of services including assistance with government processes, transparency initiatives, policy development oversight, community engagement programs, and legal guidance on governance matters.
How can I access public records?
Public records can be accessed through our online portal, by submitting a formal request at any of our offices, or through the Florida Sunshine Law portal. Most records are available within 3-5 business days.
