Florida Governor Application Guidelines

Complete guide to successfully submitting your Governor application for the Florida roleplay community.

Application Process Guide

Follow these guidelines to ensure your application is complete and meets all requirements

Introduction

Welcome to the Florida Governor Application process. These guidelines are designed to help you understand the application requirements, submission process, and evaluation criteria. Please read this document carefully before starting your application.

APPLICATION REVIEWER

Jason "Star" Shepherd

State Executive Director & Head of Department

With over 8 years of real-world leadership experience

Important Note

This application is for a roleplay community. All information provided should be appropriate for this context. We value creativity, dedication, and a genuine interest in contributing to our community.

AI & Assistance Policy

STRICTLY PROHIBITED

Using any AI tools, writing assistants, or external sources to help answer questions or improve your text is strictly forbidden and will result in immediate department blacklisting.

All applications must be your own original work. We can detect AI-generated content, and any attempt to use such tools will permanently disqualify you from all future department applications and activities.

What's Allowed

  • Your own original thoughts and writing
  • Basic spell-checking tools
  • Thinking through responses before writing
  • Taking breaks during the application process

What's Not Allowed

  • AI writing assistants (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.)
  • Grammar improvement tools beyond basic spelling
  • Copying content from other sources
  • Having someone else write your responses
  • Using templates or pre-written answers

Eligibility Requirements

Community Requirements

  • Must be at least 13 years of age
  • Must have a clean roleplay record with no major infractions
  • Active participation in the community is highly valued
  • Previous government roleplay experience is preferred but not required

Platform Requirements

  • Valid Discord account with appropriate username
  • Active Roblox account in good standing
  • Regular activity on both platforms
  • No history of platform bans or suspensions

Important Notice

Applications that do not meet these basic eligibility requirements will be automatically disqualified. Please ensure you meet all criteria before applying.

Application Process

Application Timeline

The application period closes on November 10, 2025 at 7:30 PM EST. Late submissions will not be accepted. The review process typically takes 1-2 hours after submission, after which all applicants will be notified of the committee's decision.

Application Sections Guide

Personal Information

Provide accurate personal details including your name and contact information. We only need your phone number or email if critical things happen and we need to get a hold of you through alternative means - otherwise we will contact you through Discord.

Privacy Note: Your contact information is kept confidential and used only for essential communications related to your application.

Discord Information

Include your complete Discord ID and username. Be honest about your activity level as this helps us understand your availability. This will be our primary method of communication.

Note: Your Discord account will be verified during the review process.

Roblox Information

Provide your Roblox ID and username. Detail your experience level and any relevant roleplay background.

Tip: Mention specific government or leadership roleplay experience if applicable.

Roleplay Experience

This section is crucial. Be detailed about your roleplay background, experience, and record. Honesty is valued over perfection.

Important: Be truthful about any past infractions. We value transparency and growth.

Leadership Questions

Demonstrate your understanding of leadership principles and how you would apply them in a roleplay government context.

Tip: Provide specific examples when possible to illustrate your points.

Governance Vision

Share your vision for Florida's future in our roleplay community. Be creative but realistic in your proposals.

Note: We're looking for candidates who understand both the creative and administrative aspects of roleplay governance.

Situational Questions

These questions test your problem-solving skills and judgment in challenging scenarios relevant to government roleplay.

Tip: Consider both the immediate response and long-term implications in your answers.

Advanced Questions

Demonstrate your depth of understanding about governance, policy, and your potential legacy as Governor.

Note: These questions separate exceptional candidates from good ones. Take your time with them.

Response Quality Guidelines

Writing Quality

  • Use proper grammar and spelling
  • Write in complete sentences and paragraphs
  • Be clear and concise in your responses
  • Avoid excessive slang or informal language
  • Proofread your answers before submission

Content Quality

  • Provide detailed, thoughtful responses
  • Use specific examples to support your points
  • Demonstrate creativity and original thinking
  • Show understanding of roleplay governance concepts
  • Balance idealism with practical considerations

Length Guidelines

  • Short answer questions: 2-4 sentences minimum
  • Essay questions: 1-2 paragraphs minimum
  • Use the character counters as a guide, not a limit
  • Quality over quantity - be substantive but concise
  • Ensure you fully answer the question being asked

Pro Tip

The most successful applications typically spend 60-90 minutes completing the form. Take your time to craft thoughtful, detailed responses that showcase your qualifications and vision.

Fair Evaluation Process

All applications are reviewed by State Executive Director Jason "Star" Shepherd with over 8 years of real-world leadership experience. We use a standardized scoring system to ensure fairness and consistency. The committee considers both the content of your responses and your overall fit for the Governor role in our specific community context.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

Application Errors

  • Incomplete applications: Ensure every required field is filled
  • Vague responses: Be specific and detailed in your answers
  • Poor grammar/spelling: Proofread carefully before submitting
  • Missing deadlines: Submit before the closing date and time
  • False information: Be honest about your experience and background
  • AI-generated content: Immediate department blacklist offense

Content Issues

  • Generic responses: Tailor your answers to our specific community
  • Overpromising: Be realistic in your goals and proposals
  • Ignoring questions: Answer what is actually being asked
  • Copying others: All responses must be your original work
  • Inappropriate content: Maintain professionalism throughout
  • External assistance: No AI tools or writing assistants allowed

Automatic Disqualifications

Applications will be automatically disqualified for: using AI tools or writing assistants, providing false information, plagiarism, inappropriate content, harassment of committee members, or attempting to circumvent the application process.

After Submission

Communication

  • Your submission will be posted in our Discord server
  • The review process takes approximately 1-2 hours
  • All applicants will be notified of the committee's decision via Discord
  • Selected candidates will be contacted directly
  • Check Discord regularly for updates

Follow-up Process

  • No interviews or chat follow-ups will be conducted
  • No additional questions will be asked after submission
  • Your application stands on its own merits
  • All decisions are final based on your submitted application
  • Final decisions are communicated via Discord

Questions?

If you have questions about the application process or guidelines, please review the FAQ page first. If your question isn't answered there, you can contact the application committee through our Discord server.
